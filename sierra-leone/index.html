Why are we going?

We are travelling, together with our sister(-in-law) Jill, to volunteer with Christ Centred Ministries, Sierra Leone (CCMSL) on a project which aims to build, equip and develop a centre for worship, education and training for the local community.

A decade-long civil war left many displaced people living on the outlined areas of the capital city Freetown. There is a lack of much needed services and amenities outside the city – schools, churches, skills training facilities, health, electricity, water, etc – against the backdrop of a rapidly growing population.

Where are we going?

The CCMSL project is located about 20 minutes from Freetown, Sierra Leone’s capital city. Sierra Leone is a country in West Africa that is bordered by Guinea to the northeast, Liberia to the southeast, and the Atlantic Ocean to the southwest. The country has a tropical climate, with a diverse environment ranging from savannah to rainforests and an estimated population of 6 million.
